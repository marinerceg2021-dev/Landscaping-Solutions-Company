Who We Serve

Three groups who qualify
for free service

If you or someone you love falls into one of these groups, you may be eligible to receive completely free bi-weekly lawn maintenance , no cost, ever.

Veterans
Always Free

Those who served our country deserve to come home to a yard they're proud of. We provide free bi-weekly lawn care with no questions asked.

  • Any branch of military service
  • Active duty or retired
  • Greater Pittsburgh area
First Responders
Always Free

Police, firefighters, paramedics , the people who run toward danger so the rest of us don't have to. The least we can do is keep your grass cut.

  • Police officers & sheriff deputies
  • Firefighters & paramedics
  • Active or retired first responders
Seniors 65+
Always Free

Age shouldn't mean losing the yard you've worked for your whole life. We show up every two weeks so you never have to worry about it again.

  • 65 years of age or older
  • Homeowners in Pittsburgh area
  • No income requirement
The Process

Simple as a phone call.

1

2
Tell Us About You

We'll have a quick conversation to confirm you qualify. That's it , no lengthy process.

3
We Schedule a Visit

We'll come out, assess your yard, and get you on the bi-weekly schedule at no cost.

4
Enjoy Your Yard

We show up every two weeks like clockwork. You never see a bill. You just enjoy your yard.
