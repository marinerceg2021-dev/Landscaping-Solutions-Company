Our Community Mission

The Cuts For Vets Program

"More than a business — a commitment to our community."

From day one, giving back has been at the heart of everything we do. Our Cuts For Vets program provides completely free bi-weekly lawn care to veterans, first responders, and seniors 65+ across Greater Pittsburgh. No bills. No catches. Just neighbors taking care of neighbors.

This isn't a marketing campaign — it's our mission. We've already helped multiple veterans and elderly community members who've reached out wanting to pay it forward for others. That's the Pittsburgh spirit, and we're proud to be a part of it.
